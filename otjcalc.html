Instructions and How-To:

1. View Standard Details

Use the view an apprenticeship standard selector to view key information about your chosen standard. This may be useful in highlighting whether the planned duration of your inputted dates correlate with the recommended duration according to IfATE.

2. Input your values

Use the calculator to calculate the minimum OTJH required for your standard. The calculator requires you to enter the start date, end date and weekly working hours. Be careful on what you put in to these fields as this can affect the accuracy of the calculation output.

3. Push the Button

Once you have entered your values, press the calculate button to action the calculations and the minimum OTJH will be displayed in the green field, marked as I. You can use the rest button to put the calculator back to the default should you need to.
