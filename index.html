ApprentiCalc

Calculate your minimum UK apprenticeship OTJ hours online

Navigating the requirements for apprenticeship On-The-Job (OTJ) hours can often feel complex and confusing. ApprentiCalc is designed to cut through the complexity and provide a clear, straightforward solution for both apprentices and employers.

This intuitive online tool empowers users to quickly and accurately calculate the minimum OTJ hours mandated for their specific apprenticeship programme.

By simplifying this crucial aspect of apprenticeship planning and management, ApprentiCalc helps ensure compliance with regulations and provides a transparent framework for tracking progress.
